At Generation Bamboo, we hate plastic straws, they can take over 200 years to decompose and cause harm to our environent and the animals that live in them. Bamboo is one of the worlds fastest growing plants, reaching maturity in just 3-5 years. It requires minimal input if any to grow and has unique natural bio agents at the molecular level which prevent the need for pesticides in most cases.

Card

Bamboo straws are sturdy and reusable unlike the popular disposable paper straw. You will not find the straw unusable part way through your delicious beverage and our straws are reusable over and over again. Whilst paper straws are a better solution than plastic it does feel like continuing to use any form of disposable straw is not completely solving the problem as energy needs to then go into recycling and reproducing the straw again. Reusable straws are much better for the environment and our Bamboo straws can be used for many months before needing to be replaced

Metal

Metal straws whilst being reusable are a conductor of heat, this proves problematic when drinking either hot or cold beverages. This leaves the tepid drink in the sweet spot however with the chemicals and energy required to produce metal straws it yet again provides a partial solution only. Silicone

Silicone is a man made, manufactured material. Whilst these straws are also reusable they are themselves not environmentally friendly in terms of the material they are made from and should they find themselves into landfill or the oceans we have done a full loop back to the dreaded disposable plastic straw. If our Bamboo straws sadly were to make it to landfill or the ocean they would degrade as a natural product and pose no harm to the environment or wildlife
