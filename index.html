Eco-Friendly Reusable Bamboo Straws

Join the Bamboo Generation

As you might have guessed we love bamboo and we think you will to. It's natural, sustainable, biodegradeable, and makes amazing reusable drinking straws for any drink, hot or cold.

Plastic drinking straws are everywhere, in fact the UK uses over 8 billion of them a year, and as they take over 200 years to decompose they're going to be here for a long time.

So we think it's time for a change, a new generation focused on making big differences through small changes, one of them being replacing disposable straws with bamboo straws.

Bamboo straws are natural, environmentally friendly, biodegradeable and reusable. Our straws come in three different sizes so they are perfect for all types of drinks from a cup of coffee, a martini, or a bubble tea!

Our straws come from Vietnam where the bamboo is naturally, ethically and responsible grown. They are manufactured without any chemicals or pesticides. This makes our straws as natural as they can possibly be.

50% of our profits go to charity to support long term sustainable change. 25% goes go towards reducing the plastic in our oceans and 25% goes towards supporting the education for Vietnamese children in need.
